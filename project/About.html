About Ngul~Yoen

Ngul Yoen is the project aimed at providing insights into the banking sector in Bhutan.

This proejct gives a deeper insights into customer banking choices and the factors affecting those choices (such as trust, satisfaction, service quality, accessibility and ease of use). It also focus solution driven and result oriented outcome. The main aim of the project is to get clear insight into customer banking choice and patterns, and helping bank idenify the areas for improvement and the opportunities for incorporating additional services.
